London is the capital city of the United Kingdom and is located in the southeast. It has a population of 8.9 million people

The city of London has a very rich history having been founded by the Romans as Londinium in the 1st century ad.

There are many famous landmarks in London. For example, the Big Ben, the Houses of Parliament, the Shard, Buckingham Palace

There are also a variety of places to learn about London's history, culture, art and science such as the National Gallery, Tate Modern, Natural History Museum, British Museum.

London is also famous for its Red double decker buses and having first ever underground railway in the world the London Underground
